Nazarban Iran-First AI Consulting & Delivery

EN FA

Vision (3–5 Years)

Nazarban will be Iran's reference brand for KPI-driven, secure AI Consulting & Delivery, moving enterprises from data to decisions in ≤21 days, then scaling via reusable modules and managed MLOps.

Why Now

Iranian enterprises hold years of ERP/POS/CRM data yet lack decision intelligence. Open-source CV/ML/NLP has matured, On-Prem/Hybrid meets sovereignty and risk needs, and inflationary pressure demands ROI in weeks, not months.

Operating Model (7+14)

  • 7-Day Data Readiness Audit → map KPIs, data quality, feasibility.
  • 14-Day PoC on the client's stack proving lift on real KPIs.
  • Secure by Design: On-Prem/Hybrid, RBAC, audit logs, NDA/DPA.
  • Product-Led Consulting: modular CV/ML/NLP + Nazarban Insight Panel.
  • MLOps: monitoring, drift detection, retraining, SLA dashboards.

Initial Scope

  • Computer Vision: share of shelf, planogram compliance, OOS detection.
  • Forecasting & Optimization: demand/promo, inventory and routing.
  • Persian NLP: claims/invoice extraction, ticket triage, sentiment.
  • Executive Dashboards: before/after KPI tracking and alerts.

3-Year Objectives

20–30 enterprise logos, PoC→Rollout >50%, NRR >110%, annual Nazarban AI Report, default shortlist vendor for secure On-Prem/Hybrid AI.

Success Metric

Time-to-Value proven on executive KPIs within 21 days.

Call to Action

Start with a 7-Day Data Readiness Audit and an executable 14-Day PoC.
